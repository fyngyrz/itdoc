11.4.61 - Rotate 90°

Scripting command: rot90 [Mode=0...2]

Note: All available scripting operations are listed on the Script operator page.

Usual effects. Watch out for aspect ratio when rotating regions within an image — you can use the square aspect guide with the rectangle area selection tool to show you that your selection is not going to distort the image.

Tip: If you're looking to rotate the content 90° within an image, this operator is what you want. But if you're looking to turn the entire image, for instance when the camera has produced a portrait image instead of a landscape image, you're looking for the quarter turn command.

See also: Rotation operator, which can rotate to any angle.
